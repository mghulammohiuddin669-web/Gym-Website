Expert Advice

PRO TIPS

🎯

Mind-Muscle Connection

Focus on feeling your chest squeeze — not just moving the weight. This alone can double your results.

📈

Progressive Overload

Add 2.5kg every 2 weeks. Your chest won't grow without giving it a reason to adapt and get stronger.

😴

Rest & Recovery

Muscles grow during rest, not training. Give 48–72 hours between chest sessions.

🥩

Protein Intake

Eat 1.6–2.2g of protein per kg of bodyweight daily. No protein = no muscle growth, period.
