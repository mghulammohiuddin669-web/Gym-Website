Expert Advice

PRO TIPS

🦵

Don't Skip Leg Day

Legs are 60% of your muscle mass. Training them releases more testosterone and growth hormone than any upper body workout.

📐

Squat Deep

Hips below parallel activates 30% more glute muscle than parallel squats. Mobility work will help you squat deeper safely.

⚖️

Balance Quads & Hamstrings

Most leg injuries come from quad dominance with weak hamstrings. Train RDLs and leg curls every leg session without fail.

😴

Recover Fully

Heavy leg training damages more muscle tissue than any other session. Sleep 8 hours, eat enough protein, and wait 72 hours before training again.
