Expert Advice

PRO TIPS

🎯

Lead With Elbows

On every pulling movement, think "elbows to your back pockets" — not hands to chest. This activates lats instead of biceps.

🤜

Use Straps

Your grip will fail before your back on heavy rows. Use lifting straps to keep focus on the target muscle, not your hands.

📐

Full Range of Motion

Let your shoulder blades spread at the top of every pull. This full stretch activates more muscle fibers for greater growth.

⚖️

Pull:Push Ratio

For every pressing session, do at least equal back work. Most people overtrain chest and underdevelop their back — balance it.
