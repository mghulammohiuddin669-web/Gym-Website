Expert Advice

PRO TIPS

⚖️

Train Both Equally

Triceps make up 2/3 of your arm. Most people overtrain biceps and underdevelop triceps — balance your training.

🎯

No Swinging

Momentum steals work from your biceps. Stand against a wall when curling to eliminate body swing entirely.

📐

Full Range of Motion

Full extension at the bottom of every curl and every pushdown. Partial reps build partial muscles.

🔥

Superset Them

Superset bicep and tricep exercises for a massive pump — they don't compete, so no rest is needed between them.
