Expert Advice

PRO TIPS

🍽️

Abs Are Made in the Kitchen

No amount of crunches will reveal abs covered in fat. Reduce body fat through diet — then the abs you've built will show.

💪

Treat Abs Like Any Muscle

Add resistance to ab exercises. Your abs need progressive overload to grow — weighted crunches and cable work beat 100 bodyweight crunches.

🎯

Brace, Don't Just Crunch

On every exercise, brace your core as if you're about to get punched. This deep core activation protects your spine and builds real strength.

🔄

Train Obliques Too

The obliques frame your abs and create the V-taper look. Russian twists and side planks are essential for a complete core.
